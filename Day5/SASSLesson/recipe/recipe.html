Surprise Matcha Balls

    Ingredients

  • 12 Medjool dates
  • 1 cup pistachios
  • 1/2 cup desiccated coconut
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons matcha powder, plus 1/2 teaspoon extra to dust (optional)
  • 26 frozen raspberries

    Directions for Making:

  1. Place dates in a medium bowl and cover with water. Stand for 1 hour. Drain and discard seeds.
  2. Process pistachios until a fine meal. Add dates, desiccated coconut, coconut oil and matcha powder and process until mixture comes together.
  3. Roll 3 level teaspoons of mixture around raspberries into balls. Dust with matcha powder (optional)
